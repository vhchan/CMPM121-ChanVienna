Controls:

W,S: Move back and forward
A,D: Change direction of player
Space: Small jump
Mouse: Angle up and down for more vision

Goal:

Get 50 mushroom points by collecting mushrooms and win the game while avoiding the devil who will kill you upon collision.

Other: 

There is a locked room with a massive mushroom in it. In order to unlock this room, find the special golden mushroom.
The massive mushroom is worth 25 mushroom points.
The game has three total scenes; a title screen that is returned to upon winning, the gameplay scene, and the death screen.
